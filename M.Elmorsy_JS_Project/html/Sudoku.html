Instructions

1- Use arrow keys to move between cells .

2- Use number keys 1,2,3,4 to insert the shape you want .

3- Use backspace if you want to delete the shape you inserted .
*note: you can't delete the 4 steady pictures that appears at game begining .

4- To win you have to insert all cells with the shapes
but every row, column and quarter must have different shapes .

5- To win you have to finish before time runs out .
*note: if the steady random pictures pattern is wrong please refresh the game .
